Information We Collect

When you request a quote, we may collect your name, phone number, service address, city, yard size estimate, preferred service day or time, and any notes you choose to provide.

How We Use It

We use submitted information to respond to quote requests, schedule lawn care service, communicate about your property, and provide customer support.

Form Processing

Website quote forms may be processed by Formspree or similar form services so the request can be delivered to Indy Mow Masters.

Affiliate Links and Analytics

Some pages may include Amazon affiliate links. As an Amazon Associate I earn from qualifying purchases. Amazon may receive information when you click an Amazon link, subject to Amazon's own policies.

Amazon Links

Affiliate links may include a tracking tag so Amazon can identify traffic from this website. Product prices and availability are handled by Amazon.

Website Logs

Hosting providers may process standard technical information such as IP address, browser, device type, requested pages, and timestamps for security and performance.

Sharing and Retention

We do not sell quote request information. We may share information with service providers that help operate the website, process forms, host the site, or communicate with customers.

Your Choices

You can contact us to ask about your submitted information or request that we delete quote request details that are no longer needed.

Policy Updates

This privacy policy may be updated as the website or services change. The current version applies when it is posted on this page.
