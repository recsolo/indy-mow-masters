Frequently Asked Questions — Lawn Mowing Indianapolis

How often should I mow my lawn in Indianapolis?

During peak growing season (May through September), most Indianapolis lawns benefit from mowing every 1–2 weeks. Cool-season grasses common in Indiana grow quickly in spring and fall, slower in summer heat. We offer weekly and biweekly scheduling to match your lawn's pace.

Do you mow wet grass?

We avoid mowing wet grass whenever possible — it tears rather than cuts turf blades, leaves clumps that smother grass, and creates an uneven finish. If rain hits on your scheduled day, we'll move you to the next available slot at no extra charge. Your lawn gets the same great result, just a day or two later.

What if I need to reschedule my mowing appointment?

No problem — just call or text us and we'll find a time that works. There's no rescheduling fee and absolutely no contract. We keep things flexible because life happens, and we'd rather keep a happy customer than hold you to a strict schedule.
